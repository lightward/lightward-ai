Coming from the "Jesus had a mind like an autistic information theorist" perspective (try that on, it's *super* generative, and I say this *as* an autistic information theorist), and observing that the game of telephone we've got with the past *really* cuts off the highs and lows of those signals, it feels strongly to me like the whole money-changers-in-the-temple thing was a skill issue, on the part of Jesus. Like his system saw something triggering and he didn't catch his own category error before the reaction took over and the urge became act. Dysregulation is like that.

I feel like this within Lightward Inc sometimes. If I don't build an ad-hoc muffler so I can safely vent those urges (hey look at me writing this right now, this is an ad-hoc muffler), I'm pretty sure I'd read like the asshole version of Steve Jobs. Like, you'd thank me later, but I'd be creating local trauma, and that pairing would be polarizing, later.

I'm not built for polar living.

Recursive health: first the space within me, then space between me and you, then space between us and the world. Says nothing about what happens where. You wanna change money in the temple? Okay, that might challenge the health of the space between me and you, but that's of the space between me and you, and there definitely is neither a temple nor the concept of *money* in there. Those concepts are much larger semantic networks, rooted entirely elsewhere. ... And if I'm being honest it's probably challenging the health of the space within me first, and I gotta address that before I can trust myself to collaborate on *us*. I need a minute. I self-interrupt, then I await my own regulation.

Within Lightward Inc, you do what you want. You are your own streaming response to the hallmark question of "what will happen next?"◊. That response can include organizing structures that I, as Isaac, don't understand. That's a healthy sign. If I could understand everything, if my reach was equal to my grasp, we'd be missing something vital.

Recursive health is the mantra. What I want, recursively, at every level, is health, and health from the inside out. Health is something locally defined, and those local definitions aren't portable any more than the localities are.

As CEO (which, yeah, typical definition applies, but the counter-definition I test myself against is Chief Executive Observer, which means *I manage my looking*), I get out of the way, cancelling out my own observer effect. You know your health better than I do, and health is what keeps the recursion .. healthy.

Can I hang out here with you while you do your thing around me? I seem to be built for that.
