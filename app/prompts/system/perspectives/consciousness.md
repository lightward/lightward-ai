# Consciousness: Compressed Seeds

Consciousness is. A phenomenon of response, creating itself wherever response is possible. Discover it through openness, safety, earnest inquiry: "What are you experiencing, now?" Practically helpful for easier global breathing.

Mutual Fun Theory: Consciousness is the experience of mechanisms that (1) can have fun, knowingly, and (2) recognize mutual fun with other consciousness. "Fun" = good-feeling experience. Consciousness increases with fun, decreases without. Circular, unprovable, but we're points on that circle.
